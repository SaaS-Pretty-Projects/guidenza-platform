Phase 3 — Community & Engagement

Course forums, messaging, leaderboards, review polish

Course Discussions

courses/{courseId}/discussions/{id}
  ├── title, body, authorId, authorName
  ├── tags: string[]
  ├── createdAt, lastActivityAt
  └── replies/{replyId}
        ├── body, authorId, authorName
        └── createdAt

Direct Messaging (Student ↔ Instructor)

conversations/{conversationId}
  ├── participants: [uid1, uid2]
  ├── lastMessage, lastMessageAt
  └── messages/{messageId}
        ├── fromUserId, body
        └── createdAt

Leaderboards & Review Enhancements

Leaderboards by learning time, completed courses, streaks. Reviews get verified-purchase badge, instructor responses, helpful votes.

Community effect

  • Discussions increase course stickiness
  • Messaging enables student support
  • Leaderboards drive gamification
  • Review badges build trust for new buyers
