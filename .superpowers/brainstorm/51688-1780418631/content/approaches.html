Approach: Phased Rollout

Given the scope (Payments + Quizzes + Community), a phased approach is safest

Phase 1 — Course Commerce (SafePay)

Scope
  • SafePay checkout flow (embed or redirect)
  • Purchase + enrollment gating (free preview, pay to unlock)
  • Course ownership records in Firestore
  • Order history + receipts

Phase 2 — Deep Learning Experience

Scope
  • Quizzes per module (MCQs, auto-grading)
  • Enhanced progress tracking (quiz scores, module mastery)
  • Polished certificate generation

Phase 3 — Community & Engagement

Scope
  • Course forums / discussions
  • Student-instructor messaging
  • Rating & review enhancements
  • Student leaderboards

Why this works

  • Shipping payments first = revenue sooner
  • Each phase is independently deployable
  • Ordered by value-to-effort ratio

Trade-off

  • Quizzes delayed until Phase 2
  • Need to design data model up-front to avoid migrations
